The Rundeck Job Identifier is used to uniquely identify a job. It could be either :
  • A job's UUID. Example : "2027ce89-7924-4ecf-a963-30090ada834f"
  • A job's reference, in the format : "project:group/job". Example : "my-project-name:main-group/sub-group/my-job-name", or "my-project-name:my-job-name" (for a job without a group)
